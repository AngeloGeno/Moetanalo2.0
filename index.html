    Moetanalo Attorneys Inc is an emerging, vibrant and proudly South African law firm working in various fields of specialty. The firm is led by a young and dynamic business-minded professional with innovative and fresh solutions to challenging legal issues.

    The firm is founded on African values and beliefs of ethics, humanity, growth and prestige. The desire to translate these values and beliefs to reality has resulted in the establishment of this firm in order to provide individual and corporate legal services.

    From inception, the firm intends to stand out as one of the most prominent legal firms positively contributing socially and ethically to our community.

    Our Values

    01
    Excellence

    We are striving for service excellence in all our endeavours, in a bid to ensure efficient service administration. As a fundamental point of departure, professional conduct is a forefront priority, and we take great pride in the manner with which we communicate with our stakeholders and conduct our business.

    02
    Professionalism

    Strict professionalism in carrying out the mandate given is maintained, confidentiality being the cornerstone of trust between our firm and the client.

    03
    Integrity

    In adhering to the highest standards of conduct and moral behavior we can maintain the highest level of ethics in all our dealings, ensuring client satisfaction and rendering quality services in the most effective and professional manner.

    04
    Social Responsibility

    We appreciate that a large population of people do not have access to legal services or knowledge of the basic principles where their rights are concerned. Our firm has committed itself to empowering communities by offering access to our pro bono services to the disadvantaged members of our society. Apart from being a niche law firm, we appreciate the need to interact and participate in community projects and activities.

    Meet Our Founder

    Founded by Nonkululeko Moetanalo, a distinguished legal professional with extensive expertise in both public and private sector legal matters. She brings exceptional leadership and management acumen to the firm, ensuring its sustainability through strategic private and public partnerships.

    Nonkululeko brings 15 years’ experience working as a legal professional with multi-national corporate entities in the mining, construction and product manufacturing industries managing risk portfolios, leading contract negotiations, applying and maintaining business operational licences and permits, facilitating external audits and executing recognised legal training.

    Nonkululeko is an admitted attorney of the High Court of South Africa. Her exceptional drafting skills and experience position her as a cornerstone of our firm's success.

    Our team of lawyers complements her vision by embodying the highest standards of professionalism. With proven expertise in negotiation, leadership, people management, and client service, they work collaboratively to establish strategic alliances that ensure the firm's long-term growth and sustainability.
